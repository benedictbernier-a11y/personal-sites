# ReAdvance

Film camera repair and resale business. We buy broken vintage film cameras (primarily Nikon FE, FM, F2), repair them, and sell them on our website and eBay.

## Business

- **Founders:** Benedict (repairs, sourcing, photography) and Laurence (pricing, inventory, sales platforms)
- **Target customer:** Film photography hobbyists, 18-35, looking for tested vintage cameras at fair prices
- **Revenue model:** Buy broken cameras ($15-40), repair, sell working ($50-200+). Costs/profits split 50/50.
- **Sales channels:** Our own website (preferred, no fees) and eBay/Mercari (13% fees)
- **Future service:** Repair appointments once skills are built up
