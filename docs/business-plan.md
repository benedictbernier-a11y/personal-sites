# Film Camera Repair & Resale — One-Page Business Plan

## What We Sell

Fully functional, tested vintage film cameras — starting with Nikon FE, FM, and F2 bodies. We buy broken or non-working cameras cheap, repair them, and sell them at full market value.

## Who Buys It

Film photography hobbyists, ages 18-35, riding the analog photography trend. They want a reliable vintage camera but don't want to gamble on untested eBay listings or pay shop prices. Our buyers are on Reddit (r/analog, r/filmphotography), Instagram, Etsy, and eBay.

## How They Find Us

- eBay and Mercari listings (where buyers already search for these cameras)
- Our own website (higher margins, no platform fees)
- Reddit communities and Instagram (film photography hashtags, before/after restoration posts)
- Word of mouth from satisfied buyers

## What We Charge

- **Buy price:** $10-40 per broken camera ("for parts" eBay listings, estate sales, thrift stores)
- **Sell price:** $60-200+ per working camera, depending on model and condition
- **Repair services (future):** $30-75 for common fixes, $75-150 for involved work
- **Target margin:** 3-5x on flips after parts and repair costs

## What It Costs Us

- **Startup (one-time):** $100-200 for tools (precision screwdriver set, spudgers, light seal foam/cord, lens cleaning supplies) and first batch of broken cameras
- **Per-camera costs:** $10-40 purchase + $5-15 in parts (light seals, cleaning supplies) = $15-55 per unit
- **Platform fees:** ~13% on eBay, ~10% on Mercari, $0 on our own site
- **Ongoing:** Shipping supplies, occasional replacement parts
- **Split:** All costs and profits split 50/50 between Benedict and Laurence

## How We Deliver It

1. **Source:** Buy broken Nikon FE, FM, and F2 bodies from eBay "for parts" listings, estate sales, and thrift stores
2. **Diagnose & Repair:** Benedict handles repairs — shutter advance fixes, light seal replacements, shutter cleaning, general CLA (clean, lubricate, adjust)
3. **Test:** Run a test roll through each camera to verify it works
4. **Photograph & List:** Product photos, honest descriptions, list on eBay + our website
5. **Ship:** Pack carefully, ship within 1-2 business days of sale
6. **Track:** Laurence tracks inventory, buy/repair/sell prices, and profit per unit

### Roles

- **Benedict:** Sourcing broken cameras, repairs, product photography
- **Laurence:** Pricing research, inventory tracking, sales platforms, profit analysis

### Month 1 Goal

Buy 5-10 broken cameras, repair and flip them, target $200-400 in revenue.
