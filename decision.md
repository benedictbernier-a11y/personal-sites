We've decided to start a camera repair business.

## Why
Benedict likes cameras and already has experience repairing Nikon bodies
(F2, FE, FM — shutter advance repairs). Laurence likes to repair things
and is interested in learning. We get paid a good amount after starting.

## What we do
- Buy broken film cameras on eBay, repair them, and flip them on our own website
- Price competitively — cheaper than usual while still making good value
- Eventually add repair appointments as a service once we're skilled enough

## Target customer
Film photography hobbyists — young people (18-35) riding the analog
trend who want a working vintage camera at a fair price. They're the
largest group, easiest to reach online, and actively searching for
exactly what we'd be selling.

## Business structure
- Costs and profits split 50/50
- Estimated startup cost: $100-200 (tools + first batch of cameras)
